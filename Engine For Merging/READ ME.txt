//////// CONTROLS: ////////

Camera movement:
Click and hold LMB while moving mouse to rotate camera.
Scroll to zoom.

Moving figure:
w = move up (y+)
s = move down (y-)
d = move right (x+)
a = move left (x-)
e = rotate right
q = rotate left

Animation:
P = animate between figure and square 

Exit:
ESC - closes app
